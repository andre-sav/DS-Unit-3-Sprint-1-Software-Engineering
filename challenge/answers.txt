The most important part of code reviews is to ascertain that everything functions as intended in the most efficient manner possible. Something to pay attention to in addition to this is the readability and clarity of said code. Having clear and concise code is paramount to it being maintainable when updates are needed. Descriptive variable names are also something to strive for as they add to the overall interpretability. 

Containers can help improve a situation where many computers are running slightly different things all the time by providing a way to keep track of the differences. They allow for controlled experiments and simpler integration. Logging can be easily automated and traceback becomes streamlined. Also, deploying projects and sharing is relatively straightforward with containers.
